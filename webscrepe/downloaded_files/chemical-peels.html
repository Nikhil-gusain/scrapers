Chemical peels

Rejuvenating Your Skin with Chemical Peels in Mumbai & Gurgaon

Looking for a way to reveal a smoother, brighter complexion? Looklush in Mumbai and Gurgaon offers chemical peels – a popular treatment that can rejuvenate your skin and address a variety of concerns.

What are Chemical Peels?

Chemical peels are controlled applications of acidic solutions that exfoliate the top layers of skin. This process encourages cell renewal, revealing a fresher, smoother appearance.

Benefits of Chemical Peels:

  • Reduced fine lines and wrinkles: Peels can improve skin texture and minimise the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Improved acne and acne scars: Certain peels can help clear breakouts and minimise the appearance of acne scars.
  • Reduced sun damage: Peels can address sun damage like hyperpigmentation and uneven skin tone.
  • Enhanced skin texture and clarity: Peels promote smoother, brighter, and clearer skin.

Types of Chemical Peels at Looklush:

  • Superficial Peels: Gentlest option, ideal for mild concerns like uneven skin tone and dry skin.
  • Medium Peels: Address deeper concerns like wrinkles, acne scars, and sun damage.
  • Deep Peels: Most intensive option, suitable for severe sun damage and wrinkles (performed by a dermatologist).

Choosing the Right Peel:

Our experienced team at Looklush will assess your individual needs and skin type to recommend the most suitable peel for you. We offer various peels from trusted brands, ensuring safety and effectiveness.

What to Expect:

  • Consultation: Discuss your concerns and goals with our team to determine the appropriate peel.
  • Preparation: You may receive instructions on pre-peel skincare routines and sun protection.
  • Procedure: The chosen solution is applied to the skin, followed by neutralisation and post-treatment care.
  • Recovery: Depending on the peel depth, you may experience mild redness, peeling, and sensitivity for a few days.
